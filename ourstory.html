Where It All Began

A Simple Dream.
A Warm Cup.

It started with a single question: what would the perfect coffee shop feel like? Not just the coffee — but the chairs, the light, the sound of rain on the windows, the barista who remembers your name.

In 2010, our founders rented a small corner space on Coffee House Street and opened their doors with three espresso blends, six tables, and an unshakeable belief that great coffee has the power to bring people together.

Word spread not through advertising, but through neighbors telling neighbors. Within a year, that corner space became the heart of the community — and the dream kept growing.

Our Journey

Milestones That Shaped Us

Every chapter brought new lessons, new flavors, and deeper roots in the community we love.

2010

The First Pour

Coffee House opens its doors on a rainy Tuesday morning. Forty cups sold. One couple returns the same evening — they've been regulars ever since.

2013

Sourcing Our Own Beans

We travel to Ethiopia and Colombia to meet the farmers behind our beans. Our first direct-trade partnership begins — quality and ethics, hand in hand.

2016

The Barista Academy

We launch an in-house training program for aspiring baristas, giving local youth a craft and a career. Over 200 graduates to date.

2019

Three Locations, One Soul

We open our second and third cafés, each with its own character but the same unwavering commitment: every cup matters.

2024

Still Brewing, Still Dreaming

A new roastery, a seasonal menu celebrating local harvests, and a community events calendar that fills up months in advance. The story continues.

What We Stand For

More Than Coffee

The principles that guide every decision, from the beans we source to the way we greet you at the door.

Quality Beans

We source only from farms that share our standards — sustainable, traceable, and treated with respect for the land and the people who tend it.

Community First

Coffee House was born from community and continues to give back — through events, scholarships, local partnerships, and open doors for everyone.

Craft & Care

Every drink is made to order, by hand, by a barista who takes genuine pride in the pour. No shortcuts. No compromises. Just coffee done right.
